Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a classic chinese recipe that is best made with day old rice, some fill of protein and veggies. It is a simple recipe that can be made with a wok, a frying pan, or a griddle! Just throw in what you have in the fridge and viola, a proper dish!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste
